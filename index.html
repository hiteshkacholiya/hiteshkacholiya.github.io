About Me

I am currently working as Staff Enterprise (Cloud Solutions) Architect with Baker Hughes. I am part of the Enterprise Architecture - Digital Technology in Baker Hughes.


I am an experienced IT professional with over 12 years of industry in delivering complete solutions and automations using Microsoft Technology Stack - Microsoft Azure, Microsoft SharePoint, Microsoft Office 365, .NET/SPFx/.NET Core Development Platform and Terraform Enterprise.


I cherish learning new stuff, validating learnings with certifications, automating processes & operations, being part of digital transformations, providing more self-serve capacity to end users & enhancing overall user experience by designing and implementing complex solutions using the latest technologies available.
